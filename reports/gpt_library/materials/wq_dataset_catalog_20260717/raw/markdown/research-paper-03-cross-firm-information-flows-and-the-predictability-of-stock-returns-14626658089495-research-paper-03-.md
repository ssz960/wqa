# Research Paper 03: Cross-Firm Information Flows and the Predictability of Stock Returns


Source: https://support.worldquantbrain.com/hc/en-us/community/posts/14626658089495-Research-Paper-03-Cross-Firm-Information-Flows-and-the-Predictability-of-Stock-Returns


Abstract :

We identify all return leader-follower pairs among individual stocks using Granger
causality regressions. Thus-identified leaders can reliably predict their followers' returns
out of sample, and the return predictability works at the level of individual stocks rather
than industries. Our results indicate that, independent of its size, any  rm may emerge
as a return leader by being at the center of an important news development that has
rami cations for other  rms. Indeed, stocks undergoing news-generating developments
see an increase in the number of stocks whose returns they lead.
